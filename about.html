About RCCG Zion's threshold

Welcome to RCCG Zion's threshold. We are a part of the global Redeemed Christian Church of God, with a vision to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the world. Our parish is dedicated to spiritual growth, community service, and empowering believers to fulfill their divine purpose.

Founded in 2025, our parish has been a beacon of hope and a source of spiritual transformation in the community.
